## 💡 Core Research Hypotheses & Investigations

### 1. Starchild (WOOFi) Cross-Chain Solver Swarms
*   **Hypothesis**: Coordinated agent swarms competing in real-time auctions can execute cross-chain asset routing more efficiently than traditional automated market makers (AMMs), eliminating slippage and bridging delays.
*   **Verdict**: Validated. Solvers shield users from gas complexities and cross-chain delays by bidding on intents, shifting execution complexity away from the client.
*   **Positioning**: Maturity `+75` (Active Beta), Autonomy `+48` (Dependent on user-signed intent constraints).

### 2. Intent-Based DeFi Solver Agents
*   **Hypothesis**: The dominant blockchain interface is shifting from active transaction generation to passive intent declarations, where AI agents act as arbitrageurs competing for fee extraction.
*   **Verdict**: Validated. The growth of CowSwap and UniswapX demonstrates that delegating pathfinding to specialized solver agents reduces trade costs and prevents frontrunning (MEV protection).
*   **Positioning**: Maturity `+25` (Production Utility but early AI integration), Autonomy `+64` (High execution flexibility).

### 3. Agentic MPC Key Management & ERC-4337 Wallets
*   **Hypothesis**: Multi-Party Computation (MPC) combined with Smart Accounts (ERC-4337) allows autonomous agents to manage cryptographic assets securely without private key leakage or unrestricted spending.
*   **Verdict**: Validated. Splitting keys and enforcing programmatic account abstraction policies (e.g. spending limits, white-listed vaults) enables agents to act as sovereign economic actors within strict guardrails.
*   **Positioning**: Maturity `+80` (Mature SDKs), Autonomy `+65` (Programmatically restricted autonomy).

### 4. Autonomous Yield-Optimizer Swarms
*   **Hyponomy/Hypothesis**: Autonomous multi-agent swarms can capture DeFi yields across networks faster and more profitably than manual capital allocators by instantly rebalancing pools in response to rate changes.
*   **Verdict**: Semi-validated. While yield capture is highly optimized, the risk profiles (smart contract bugs, liquidity locks, high bridging gas) demand strict threshold limits.
*   **Positioning**: Maturity `+60` (Active Beta), Autonomy `+28` (Conservative risk-constrained routing).
